### 7. Key Take‑aways for Traders (as of 05 Apr 2024)
1. **Liquidity is tightening:** With exchange reserves at historic lows, the market’s ability to absorb large sell orders is reduced. Watch for spikes in on‑chain inflows (e.g., sudden large deposits to exchanges) as a leading warning of impending sell pressure.  
2. **Long‑term holders are exiting:** A 0.9 M BTC reduction in LTH supply signals that “smart‑money” is taking profits – a classic bearish precursor in previous cycles.  
3. **Short‑term holder dominance:** The +1.12 M BTC rise in STH supply raises the proportion of coins held by speculative actors; this cohort typically exits first during a market downturn.  
4. **Cold‑wallet accumulation does not guarantee stability:** While moving BTC to cold storage reduces immediate sell pressure, it also concentrates supply in large, inactive wallets that could re‑enter the market abruptly if price declines trigger liquidation or margin calls.  
5. **Watch for macro catalysts:** The same sources note that macro “strong economic indicators” in the U.S. were already dampening Bitcoin’s rally (The Block, 4 Apr 2024) – any further tightening could accelerate the bearish pressure hinted by the on‑chain metrics.
